Flow Summary

  1. Select meeting from day strip → meeting card highlights with checkmark
  2. Recording panel expands below with meeting details, audio devices, and big red "Start Recording" button
  3. "or record without a meeting" link for ad-hoc recordings (opens a mini title+attendees form)
  4. Click Start Recording → panel transitions to active state: timer, audio levels, red "Stop Recording" button
  5. Header shows recording indicator (pulsing red dot + "Recording") — visible even when scrolled down
  6. Day strip dims during recording (can't switch meetings mid-recording)
  7. Click Stop Recording → auto-uploads → navigates to /meetings/{id} with processing view
