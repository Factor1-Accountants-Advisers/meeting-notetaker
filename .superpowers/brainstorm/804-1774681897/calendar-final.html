Vertical day list in the sidebar. 7 calendar days ahead. Weekends hidden unless they have meetings. Today highlighted with blue label + "TODAY" badge. Days with meetings get brighter text and colored left-border cards. Days without meetings shown dimmed with italic "No meetings" text. Click a meeting to select it for recording.

Design details

  • Graph API window: 7 calendar days (was 8 hours)
  • Weekend days: Hidden unless they have meetings
  • Today: Blue "TODAY" badge, always shown even without meetings
  • Days with meetings: Brighter day label (white-ish), meeting cards with colored left border
  • Days without meetings: Dimmed label, italic "No meetings" text
  • Meeting cards: Left accent border, title + time + duration, clickable to select for recording
  • Selected state: Meeting card gets highlighted border (matches existing pattern)
  • Refresh button: Re-fetches from Graph API
